Welcome, nice to meet you

Our Mission at Alfred's is to provide the best Sweets & Treats to bring smiles to everyone's face!

Alfred, our master chef!

Alfred began his career as a chef at restaurant Beaucoup d'ours in Paris. After many successful years, he left to set out on his own adventure as a top pastry chef in the world. Honors include being named one the “Top 10 Pastry Chefs” in 2015 by Dessert Magazine and 2016 San Francisco Focus Magazine Pastry Chef of the Year.

Passion for Sweets & Treats since 2014

As a bakery, we offer a variety of fresh baked cupcakes, pastries, cookies, cakes, and pies. As a member of the community we offer a friendly place to enjoy some work time or meet with friends. Everything we serve is made with the finest ingredients and crafted daily with care and love.
